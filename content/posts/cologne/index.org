Had a Deutscher Akademischer Austausch Dienst orientation in the Köln/Deutz neighborhood. Some photographs from my Stadtrundgang on both days ...

The face below the town hall clock looks across at a guild building and is part of an elaborate Kölscher friendly antipathy between government and commerce. The guild building has a statue mooning the Stadthalle.
